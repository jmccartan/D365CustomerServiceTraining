# Suggested Learning Plan (Simple Bootcamp)

If you were onboarding someone to **D365 Customer Service in ~12–15 hours**, this order works well:

**Day 1 – Platform**

1. Dynamics 365 Foundations
    
2. Dynamics 365 Applications Overview
    

**Day 2 – Product**  
3. Dynamics 365 Getting Started  
4. Dynamics 365 for Customer Service

**Day 3 – Configuration**  
5. CRM Customization and Configuration  
6. Customizing with Power Apps

**Day 4 – Ecosystem**  
7. Field Service  
8. Copilot in Dynamics  
9. Customer Service Processes

# Dynamics 365 Customer Service – Expert Learning Path

## Phase 1 — Dynamics Platform Fundamentals

Goal: Understand the **platform that Customer Service runs on (Dataverse + Power Platform)**.

|Course|Platform|Time|Link|
|---|---|---|---|
|Microsoft Dynamics 365 Foundations|Pluralsight|~1 hr|[https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/microsoft-dynamics-365-foundations](https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/microsoft-dynamics-365-foundations?utm_source=chatgpt.com)|
|Microsoft Dynamics 365 Applications Overview|Pluralsight|~36 min|[https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/microsoft-dynamics-365-applications-overview](https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/microsoft-dynamics-365-applications-overview?utm_source=chatgpt.com)|
|Microsoft Dynamics 365: Getting Started|Pluralsight|~2 hr|[https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/microsoft-dynamics-365-getting-started](https://www.pluralsight.com/courses/microsoft-dynamics-365-getting-started?utm_source=chatgpt.com)|
|Introduction to Microsoft Dataverse|Microsoft Learn|~1 hr|https://learn.microsoft.com/training/modules/introduction-powerapps-common-data-service|

Outcome:

- Understand **entities, tables, relationships**
    
- Understand **model-driven apps**
    
- Understand the **Dynamics ecosystem**
